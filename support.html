Stuff People Ask A Lot

Q: Are these mods actually safe?

A: Yeah, we scan everything before we put it up. No viruses, no malware, just working mods. We test them ourselves too.

Q: Am I gonna get banned?

A: Look, we can't promise you won't get banned. That's just how it is with mods. But we try to make them as safe as possible. Use at your own risk though.

Q: How do I even install this stuff?

A: Each mod has instructions, but usually you just extract the files to your game folder and run it as admin. If you get stuck, just ask us.

Q: Do these work with the newest game updates?

A: We try to keep everything updated when games patch. Check the mod page to see what version it works with.

Q: Can I get my money back?

A: Depends on the situation. Just contact us and we'll see what we can do. We're pretty reasonable about it.

Q: How fast do you respond?
